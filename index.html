Welcome to Weatherman

This is a tool/game to generate loops of white noise for studying, sleeping, or any general escapism you may be looking for. You can draw clouds by dragging your mouse across the page, and create sounds using the different keys on your keyboard. Feel free to experiment and discover new sounds!

In the settings menu you can customize the length of the loop, as well as what is displayed on screen. You can change your environment using the buttons on the bottom bar. Happy weathering!
